Prometheus (Web Scraper):

  • Visually select elements to scrape—just point, click, and go.
  • Schedule scraping jobs to run automatically on your terms.
  • Manage and rotate proxies with ease for safer scraping.
  • Export your results in the formats you actually need: CSV, JSON, Excel, Google Sheets, and more.
  • Watch your scraping in real-time with live progress updates and a job history you can always revisit.<

Prometheus is a personal web scraping tool built to make web data collection simple, visual, and accessible—no coding required. I created Prometheus because I wanted a flexible, easy-to-use scraping solution that wasn’t locked behind paywalls or burdened with complicated setups. Most scraping tools either feel clunky, overly technical, or are designed for enterprise use. I wanted something different—a scraper that I could launch quickly, configure visually, and actually enjoy using for personal projects like price tracking, article collection, and general web research. here.
